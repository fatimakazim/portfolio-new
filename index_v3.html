FitTrack App

A comprehensive fitness tracking application designed to help users achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized workout plans, progress monitoring, and advanced health metrics visualization.

Sketch Adobe XD InVision Swift UI

Project Overview

FitTrack was developed to address the growing need for a comprehensive fitness tracking solution that combines workout planning, progress monitoring, and health metrics in one intuitive platform.

Target Audience

Fitness enthusiasts aged 25-45 who value data-driven workout planning and progress tracking

Project Duration

6 months from concept to launch

Team Size

8 members including designers, developers, and fitness experts

Design Process

Research

Conducted user interviews and market analysis to identify key pain points and opportunities

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes to establish core functionality and user flows

Visual Design

Developed high-fidelity designs with focus on accessibility and visual hierarchy

Testing

Conducted usability testing with target users to validate design decisions

Key Features

Personalized Workout Plans

  • AI-powered workout recommendations
  • Custom exercise library
  • Video demonstrations

Progress Monitoring

  • Visual progress charts
  • Achievement system
  • Progress photos

Health Metrics

  • Heart rate monitoring
  • Sleep tracking
  • Nutrition tracking
